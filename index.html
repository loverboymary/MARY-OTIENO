Role of Agriculture

Agriculture plays a vital role in providing food, raw materials, and employment globally. It is the backbone of most economies, ensuring food security and driving industrial development.

Importance of Agriculture

Agriculture supports life by offering:

  • Food production
  • Employment opportunities
  • Raw materials for industries

Benefits

  1. Improves nutrition
  2. Promotes economic stability
  3. Encourages sustainability
